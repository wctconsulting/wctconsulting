Skype for Business Performance Analysis

IT Analyst

Background

For many years Cisco Webex was used as common meeting tool at customer premises. 2015 the company decided to move away and replace Webex with Microsoft Lync. After implementation, the new meeting and collaboration platform showed weak performance and poor stability. In collaboration with Microsoft and different internal and external Service Owners the expected performance was to be established to provide all co workers with a stable and cost efficient meeting platform. Skype for Business is owned by Microsoft and one of the most commonly used virtual communication platforms.

Scope & Defined Goals

  • Ensure expected performance of Lync/Skype for Business service as online meeting and collaboration platform for the future.
  • Identify most relevant situations (locations/user groups/use cases)
  • Define performance levels by objective criteria
  • Assess performance levelfor each situation
  • Identify dependencies for each situation/performance level

Scope of my deliverables

Identification of most relevant situations (locations/user groups/use cases). All 7 customer locations where taken into consideration. The following areas were included in the analyses

  • Locations: 7 customer locations
  • Network: customers network, 4G, wireless connections, cabled connections, external participants
  • Use cases: chat, content sharing, audio, video, meetings
Define Performance Levels by objective criteria
  • Connect to meeting audio using phone
  • Connect to meeting shared content + audio using phone
  • Connect to shared content + Lync audio + Lync video (IP based)
  • Connect to shared content + Lync Audio (VoIP)
Identify dependencies for each situation/performance level and set reachable next level/end level goals for each locations
  • Level 4 should be the long term goal for ALL situations
  • Step by step plan and needed effort should be defined for each Situation
As a result the network setup was analyzed, bottleneck identified and adressed. Lync was updated with Skype for Business to ensure an uptodate application and better performance. Best practices and recommendations, based on the Office 365 enablement plan from Microsoft, have been successfully performed. Required Change Requests were filed and followed up. Next steps to further improve Skype for Business and keep it up to date were planned.
