E-Commerce Magento

Thesis, Content Management, Infrastructure Management, Webdesign

Background

This project work deals with the selection and construction of an e-commerce shop for fashion jewelery and accessories. With the help of Web 2.0, the two economy factors work and capital are examined. On one hand to generate a regular income and on the other hand to promote creativity in the field of design and fashion. Taking into account the central question:"Which criteria must be met for a webshop to work profitably" the following goals were derived.
  • The webshop should be set up cost-effectively in a timeframe of 6 months
  • Administration and development should be simple and time-optimized.
  • With reference to best practices, usability serves as basis for construction.
The thesis covers e-commerce and online marketing tools as a critera for selecting technical solutions and tools for implementing the website.

Scope of transformation

The thesis was build into three main parts. Part I was the theoretic background elaboration of the topics Online Marketing and E-Commerce. Part II was to be seen as the practical transformation of theoretic knowledge. Part III contained a summary of the results. Strategic WebDesign, Decisions on themes and tools had to be taken. Research in relevant content was essential to create an attractive website to serve the purpose. Online research as well as offline book sources have been used for creating, reference and documentation of the webshop. In summer 2019 the web shop was taken offline to be upgraded to the latest available version of Magento (2.3). As intermediate solution - a static HTML5 Webpage is used.
