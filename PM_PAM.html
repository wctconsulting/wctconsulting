Product Area Manager

Background

Based on the outcome of the analysis "Managing common standard platform" the role of the Product Area Manager (PAM) had to be filled. Creating forms and processes, establishment of a change advisory board and leading the activities in a controlled way to a stable functioning infrastructure was key. The analysis was created based on ITIL guidelines which required close interaction with stakeholder from configuration management and service level management. An active role within Change Management, especially in leading in the newly established change advisory board was highly essential. The role itself was suggested to be established within the IT Delivery area. The function as a requester role followed the company's authorisation matrix.

Scope of transformation

Take responsibility in the role of the PAM with below description

  • Report, analyse and recommend on items not compliant with enterprise standards.
  • Establish and maintain process alignment with software licence management.
  • Knowledge handling within the common desktop platform.
  • Change Management of application landscape within the enterprise.
  • Review infrastructure architectural development.
  • Coordinate infrastructure updates.
  • Act as a change manager for commond desktop platform.

Scope of my deliverables

  • Interim acting as Product Area Manager
  • Establishing application change management and change advisory board. Leading and handing over to future permanent PAM.
  • Planning and executing Lifecycle Management with IT Operations.
  • Maintain software requests, plan and order software packages for automatic distribution.
  • Implementing working routines based on the outcome of the preceding analysis "Managing common standard platform"
  • Create guidelines and support material for IT Operations
  • Create handbooks and training material for company coworkers.
  • Educate and train offshore personal supporting customers infrastructure.
