Printing Services

Project Coordinator

Background

At the time several reasons where given to review and consolidate the existing printing solution on customers premises in the Netherlands. Contract endings with existing printer outsourcing partner was due. Furthermore the goal of having a global common printer environment, where users would be able to print company wide by only using their company ID card or company login credentials gave the project a kickstart.

Scope of transformation

Evaluate the current contract with existing outsourcing partner and a possible extension with the focus on implementing the easy-to-use Smartprint solution. Evaluate possible other suppliers, supporting the implementing the Smartprint solution on a better or at least same level of current support measurments. Decide and implement the new solution.

Scope of my deliverables

  • Deliver a plan of approach for the implementation
  • Technical guidance and best practices
  • Evaluate different suppliers together with stakeholders from digital workplace, main project leader and administrative stakeholders.
  • Coordinate implementation of Smartprint Solution after decision on supplier
  • Coordinate with the rollout responsible in the Netherlands
