Background

The evolving development of Web 2.0 and the changing trends of the global market required the need of widening the focus on new marketing and sales channels for local entrepreneurs, corporations, local small/medium enterprises and even non-business customers. The new Top Level Domains, including .wien, offer an immense capability of new ways presenting a company’s profile. Starting with February 2014, about 469 of the new domains arrived, released and overseen by the global non-profit organisation ICANN (Internet Cooperation for Assigned Names and Numbers).

By using the internet and its incredible possibilities in the areas of marketing, advertisement, customer acquisition and loyalty, a company with any relation to Vienna would highly increase its popularity. Wien.domains offers specialized internet services, primarily new domain registrations including providing domain strategies with focus on new TLD’s, domain catching, SEO and online marketing. To show and guide customers to succeed on the local market in Vienna by optimizing their internet presence to increase their ability to compete was the main goal.

With the new GLTD's (Generic Top Level Domains) both companies and private customers could identify themselves by profession. Domain extensions like .photography, .london, .gift, .beer, .restaurant, .wien are only few examples to name. Especially people having connections to Vienna would benefit greatly from the new TLD .wien. The trend of earning money online is greater than ever and will raise in future. Those customers can show off any kind of their blogs and business, related to Vienna with the perfect domain ending .wien.

"Be local - act global" Based on the new opportunities a startup was created to serve the niche domain .wien. Wien.domains has its heart and soul in Vienna, driven by the desire to help and support people living in Vienna succeeding with their local business. Moreover the call for international attention to the profound possibilities and beauty of Vienna. All members of the team are raised in this gorgeous city. With the drive to present Vienna to both local and international customers the team shows innovative ways to new and existing organizations. New strategies together with the most successful tools for companies presenting their business online our expertise will help customers to succeed. International corporations establishing a branch in Vienna will have a great opportunity to thrive on the local market in Vienna with the new TLD .wien by addressing their offers to the Viennese population combined with a view of their home country.

Scope of transformation

Strategic WebDesign, Decision on themes and tools had to be taken. Research in relevant content was essential to create an attractive website to customers. Different reseller portals required different tools, which resulted in the choice of using WHMCS as web hosting billing & automation platform. The system needed to be setup and integrated into the webpage, which was designed with WordPress. Content creation itself paired with SEO resulted in a high ranking of the website at its release date in June 2014.
