IT Infrastructure Standard Platform Analysis

IT Analyst

Background

For a number of years the Personal Computer (Windows or Mac) - one of the most important components of the digital workplace the customer offers to its co-workers – has been managed using the proprietary common platform. While this platform is in many ways a well-functioning and stable platform, it does imply some challenges to let support and maintain one of to most important tools depending on a proprietary technological solution not owned or controlled by the customer. The emerging expectation of the users to be able to access resources and solutions using a growing number of different device types and connection methods does in many ways highlight the shortcomings of the common platform. Support for only Windows and Mac PCs, limited ability to install useful software (apps) and very limited influence on the strategic development of the platform.

Scope of transformation

  • Gather and structure existing knowledge and analysis of the synchronization issues
  • Investigate root causes for the synch issues and possible solutions or workarounds to the avoid these problems
  • Gather, structure and verify knowledge and generally shared assumptions regarding the common infrastructure platform dependencies of customers co-workers
  • Assess on a high level the pros and cons of the common platform for the customer
  • Assess and describe on a high level what the customer needs in terms of management of end user computing devices – taking into account both asset management, life cycle management as well as software deployment and maintenance management
  • Based on findings provide recommendations regarding approach to the synchronization issues
  • Based on findings provide high level recommendations regarding alternatives or additions to the common platform as end user computing management tools

Scope of my deliverables

For the pre-study below listed techniques based on best practices of undertaking a business analysis have been combined to acquire reliable results.

  • Interviews with different stakeholder
  • Brainstorming
  • Root/Cause analysis
  • Observation
  • Document analysis
  • Focus groups
  • Interface analysis

Analysing existing business dependencies on the current Infrastructure, domain affiliates, Active Directory setup and related instabilities, Documented as-is IT and business dependencies on Domain affiliates, ICC and Active Directory. Based on the questions

  • What is the added value of the common platform for the customer?
  • Who needs the common platform?
  • What are the pro and cons of the platform?
  • Which specific operation critical resources, services, solutions can only be accessed using an common platform client device?
