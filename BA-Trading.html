IT Security Analysis

IT Security Analyst

Background

Based on global requirements to secure IT environment, guarantee business continuity and to be ISO compliant in all Trading Offices worldwide belonging to customer's enterprise, internal IT Audits were initiated. A set of documents were centrally created in order to support the audits in a standardized and structured way. The result was to be a documented analysis from an IT Security perspective. Proposal of demands and recommendation of actions for improvement in case of shortages are found, were to be presented. Interviews with different stakeholders, physical inspections of IT premises (server room), risk analysis, presentation and discussion of the result was the goal for a 2-days visit at each Trading office.

Scope of transformation

  • Prepare each Trading Office with background information
  • Present methods and project description to stakeholders
  • Physical inspection of IT premises
  • Interview with different stakeholders
  • Performing the risk analysis
  • Presentation and discussion of the results

Scope of my deliverables

Performing the IT Audits for 12 trading offices within Central Europe and South Europe. The audits were performed twice within a timeframe of 3 years, where the second audit was a follow up after 3 years. My work included the above described "scope of transformation". Preparation & planning, advising local IT personal on improvements to meet the conditions stated in the standard questionnaire, risk evaluation & classification (including risk & cost calculations) as well as business dependency evaluation was part of the process. In a final report I placed demands & recommendations of improvements to the respective offices.
