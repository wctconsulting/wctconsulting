Rollout Apple Computers

Project Leader

Background

About 25 MacIntosh machines used in different graphical departments needed to be aligned and standardized with a common desktop platform. Before the implementation of the standard setup, all machines in question had different versions of Operating System, applications, installation packages and hardware. The goal was to reach a homogenous setup on all MacIntosh computer within the company by standardizing hardware,software and deployment methods.

Scope of transformation

The project will be run as a guided process by the project leader, but will also give freedom to the team members to carry out their tasks and deliver their results in the way they can do it best. The borderlines are defined in time and scope. Users will have no choice to decide on whether they want to get a standardized environment or not. Installations are to be seen as mandatory. Exemptions will only be granted, in case there exist a business case. This business case must be signed off by the business owner.

Scope of my deliverables

  • Project definition and planning
  • Project execution, control and documentation
  • Lead and perform together with IT resources from Germany and Sweden hardware & software upgrade for main infrastructure servers as well as outdated business applications.
